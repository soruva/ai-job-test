FAQ

Is this a real prediction?
This is a lightweight directional test meant to help people think about AI exposure. It is not a guaranteed prediction of future job outcomes.
What makes a job more AI-safe?
Jobs involving empathy, creativity, human trust, leadership, and complex judgment tend to be harder to automate.
Can companies use this tool?
Yes. Some teams use tools like this as conversation starters about workforce change, skills development, and AI readiness.
