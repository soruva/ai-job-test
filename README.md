# AI Positioning

AI Positioning is a simple decision-awareness tool that helps you understand where you stand in the AI economy.

It evaluates how your role may evolve with AI and highlights your positioning score based on adaptability, leverage, and future relevance.

## What it does

- Estimates your position in the AI economy
- Highlights your exposure to automation
- Shows your relative standing compared to others

## Why it matters

AI is not just replacing jobs — it is reshaping positioning.

Understanding your position early gives you an advantage.
